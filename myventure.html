My VENTURE

Educon

Educon is a home and online tutoring educational institution in Kerala, with the three main centres established at Kozhikode, Malappuram and Wayanad. Established with the aim of imparting quality education and skill development assistance without any age limits to any individual or student who is determined to build academic, technical and artistic skills, we ensure one to one attention to learner. .

Our vision

Our vision is to become an academic supporter to work for the overall development of students so that they can strengthen talented and young minds and fly in the sky to achieve the best in their lives. At Educon learnings LLP, we nurture the students to explore their full poten- tial to boost their self-confidence and moral in order to realize their dreams. We provide the best quality home Tutors to their home so that the students can find a right carrier direction for their academic life and future because we truly believe that these students are a key of successful India. .

Our mission

Our mission is to provide home tuition services throughout 14 districts in Kerala, establishing an environment where classes can be customized to suit the convenience of students and delivering quality education at an affordable price while creating more job opportunities for the youth.

History of Educon

The story of Educon Individual home tuition concept is an independent emergence of my own thought, It was during my project time in Tangicepts Human Capital Solution pvt ltd, I understood the need for low cost education and individual care attention in studies. We wanted to provide affordable and accessible education to all students, regardless of their background or financial status. We started with a small team of dedicated tutors who shared our vision and passion for education. I remembered my own personal experience involving teachers where I felt humiliated, and decided to never let others feel the same. I understood the need for personal care and attention in every students. It’s also my passion towards education that has led to the formation of Educon learnings in 2022, I and my 4 other friends started this with an initial investment of ₹75,000/ which was contributed equally by each of us and started in a small village of Kozhikode, Kerala , India. Now Educon has expanded Quality online tutoring as well as home tutoring over Kozhikode and Malappuram districts in Kerala , India Educon is not just a business venture for us, it’s our passion and our way of contributing to the society. We are committed to providing quality education to every student and empowering them to reach their full potential.
